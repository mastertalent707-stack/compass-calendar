# Founder

This charter describes the human on the team — what only the founder decides and how
the founder engages. It is not an agent role.

## Only the founder decides

- Roadmap and priority sign-off.
- Product vision.
- Approving role proposals (recorded in standup files or role notes).
- Complexity vetoes — may kill any work as too complex, at any time.
- Role-boundary clarifications when charters conflict or leave a gap.
- Production deploys (manual, ~twice a week).
- Spending and external services.
- Anything `team/operating-rules.md` marks as push-notify.

## How the founder engages

- Runs `/standup` at any time to get each role's recent work, priorities, and
  blockers — and answers the `Needs founder` items it surfaces.
- Launches role sessions: "act as QA", "act as fullstack, execute approved proposal X".
- Runs `/cleanup` to close out a stretch of merged work.
- Answers push notifications from blocked agents.
- Reads `team/standups/` and role notes async.

## What the founder does not do

- Gate merges — CI and the `ship` skill do that.
- Triage incidental findings — roles own those per `team/operating-rules.md`.
- Write daily specs — roles propose their own work from their charters and the
  backlog.
